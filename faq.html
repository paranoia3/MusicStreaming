Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions here.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time in your profile settings. Go to the "Subscription" section and follow the on-screen instructions.

Is it possible to download tracks?

Yes, with an AITUMusic Premium subscription you can download an unlimited number of tracks and listen to them offline.

What devices is the service available on?

AITUMusic is available on smartphones (iOS and Android), tablets, computers via a web browser, as well as smart speakers and TVs.

What is a family subscription?

A family subscription allows you and up to five family members to enjoy all the benefits of Premium for one great price. Each member will have their own separate account.
